# SplitSavvy — Split Bill & Payment Tracker Web App

Source: https://krackeddevs.com/code/bounty/split-bill-payment-tracker-web-app

## Summary
Build a Split Bill + Payment Tracker app that helps an organizer collect shared payments without awkward follow-ups.

- Difficulty: intermediate
- Status: ONGOING
- Deadline: Monday, 1 June 2026, 3:59 PM MYT
- Reward: RM500

## About
The app should allow an organizer to create a bill, share a payment link, and track who has paid and who has not.

Use cases include: group meals, events, trips, house bills, sports sessions, class activities, or community collections.

Real payment gateway integration is **not required** — a manual or simulated payment flow is accepted.

Design-wise, builders are encouraged to create a unique theme/brand/concept (fintech feel, student group tool, makan tracker, premium event dashboard, etc.). The goal is something simple, useful, and visually memorable.

## Main Criteria
### Core Functionality
- Organizer can create a bill
- Bill includes: title, amount, participants, due date, description
- App generates a shareable bill/payment link
- Members can view the bill and confirm payment
- Organizer can track paid and unpaid members
- Dashboard shows: total collected, remaining amount, payment progress

### User Experience
- Easy to understand for both organizer and members
- Clear flow from creating a bill to tracking payments
- Mobile-friendly (most users open links from WhatsApp)
- Simple enough for real people to use without explanation

### Design & Creativity
- Clean, polished, visually appealing UI
- Creative theme/branding encouraged
- Dashboard should feel useful and satisfying
- Bonus points: strong layout, animations, micro-interactions, dark mode, unique visual direction
